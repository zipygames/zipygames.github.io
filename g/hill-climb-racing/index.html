Hill Climb Racing Lite

Hill Climb Racing Lite is a lightweight HTML5 version of the wildly popular 2D physics-based mobile game Hill Climb Racing. Drive uphill across impossible terrain featuring steep hills, deadly valleys, and treacherous jumps. The game combines non-realistic physics with coin-collecting chaos, creating an addictive challenge for millions of players worldwide.

Developed by Fingersoft, this Lite version offers faster loading, fewer lags, and cross-device support, making it perfect for quick sessions on desktop or mobile devices. With portrait mode on mobile and smooth gameplay, hill climbing has never been easier.


New Update: World Tour Levels

The World Tour mode introduces a series of hand-crafted levels, each with unique finish lines and challenges. Pick vehicles, climb stages, and unlock new worlds while enjoying Cartapult — a feature that lets you launch back into the run from crashes without starting over.

  • Worlds include: Countryside, Seasons, Moon, Super Countryside, Neon
  • Cartapult allows you to continue momentum after crashes

Gameplay & Controls

The controls are simple yet essential for mastering physics-based driving:

  • Desktop: Press D / right arrow to accelerate, A / left arrow to brake
  • Mobile: Tap right side to accelerate, left side to brake

When airborne, these controls also rotate your vehicle forward or backward, allowing you to control landings and survive longer.


Gameplay: Obstacles & Challenges

Drive as far as possible while collecting coins, avoiding fuel depletion, and keeping Bill Newton safe. Terrain includes brutal hills, valleys, jumps, mud, ice, bridges, and massive gaps. Landing wrong or mismanaging momentum can flip your vehicle and end your run.

  • Fuel canisters and batteries help survival
  • Impossible jumps require precise landing angles
  • Downhill stretches test control at high speed
  • Each crash teaches physics, momentum, and rotation

Vehicles & Upgrades

Start with a Jeep and unlock vehicles such as:

  • Monster Truck – crush obstacles
  • Motocross Bike – agile maneuvers
  • Tank – unstoppable power
  • Moon Lander – lunar terrain
  • Santa's Sleigh – festive hill climbing

Upgrade through the Garage system:

  • Engine upgrades – more power and acceleration
  • Suspension improvements – smoother landings
  • Tire upgrades – better grip on difficult terrain
  • 4WD systems – conquer steeper inclines

Earning Extra Coins

  • Air Time – longer jumps earn more coins
  • Back Flip / Front Flip – perform clean flips for bonus coins
  • Neck Flip – rare landing on back for extra coins (ends run)

Two Ways to Lose

  • Run out of fuel – miss too many canisters or drive aggressively
  • Break Bill Newton's neck – crash or land incorrectly

Both result in instant respawn at the current stage, allowing you to try again with lessons learned.


Who Created Hill Climb Racing?

Finnish programmer Toni Fingerroos developed Hill Climb Racing solo in 2012 under the Fingersoft studio. It became a global phenomenon, amassing millions of downloads and establishing Fingersoft as a major indie success story.
