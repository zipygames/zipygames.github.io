Master Chess

Master Chess is a classic board game that challenges your strategic thinking and planning skills. You can play online against the computer or a friend, locally or offline, making it a versatile game for every chess enthusiast.

Built with HTML5 by Codethislab, Master Chess runs smoothly on desktop, mobile phones, and tablets. Move your pieces carefully, anticipate your opponent’s tactics, and aim for checkmate to win the game.


How to Play Master Chess

  • Use the tap (mobile) or left mouse button (desktop) to select and move pieces.
  • Plan your strategy to protect your king while capturing your opponent’s pieces.
  • Play against the computer or challenge a friend on the same device.

Game Objective

The goal is to checkmate your opponent’s king by strategically moving your pieces across the board. Each piece has unique movement rules, so learning how to combine them effectively is key to victory.


Tips & Strategy

  • Control the center of the board to dominate the game.
  • Think several moves ahead and anticipate your opponent’s strategies.
  • Protect your king while creating opportunities to capture valuable pieces.
  • Use pawns effectively to create openings and defend key positions.
